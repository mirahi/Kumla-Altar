Aim of conservation of the sculptures

Conservation of the sculptures was executed simultaneously with the conservation of the other parts of the altar-piece. The aim was to stabilize the condition of the sculptures by consolidating loose paint, gilding and gesso ground, by removing surface dirt as well as wax that had been used as a previous conservation treatment.

As an aesthetical conservation - restoration treatment the effect of visible white gesso in damaged areas was visually diminished by retouching in order for the viewer to get a more unified image of the sculptures and their gilded and painted surfaces. The altar will continue to be exhibited at the Swedish History museum after the conservation and therefore the visual, aesthetical aspect of the whole was considered in the aims of conservation.
