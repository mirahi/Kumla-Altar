Aims and goals

The project has to key aims:

  • To deepen Nordic cross-sector cooperation and expertise
  • Develop good, innovative practices for public engagement in the field of conservation education.

The project focused on public engagement, research, open-to-public conservation of the Medieval altar from Kumla, belonging to the collection of the Swedish History Museum, Stockholm and dissemination of the results of the project.
