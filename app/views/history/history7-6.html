St. Olov

Measurements
Height 95 cm
Width 23 cm
Depth 15 cm

Separate parts on the sculpture: None.

Missing parts: The tip of the nose of the human-headed dragon is missing. One piece of the carved decoration of the crown is missing from the right hand side. In St. Olov´s right hand there has been an ax, which has been lost.

Cracks in timber: On the right side of the hair, there is a crack in the timber.

Linen or parchment visible: Linen cloth is visible in the crown, under the gesso ground.

Paint layer

  • Light red: Skin.
  • Red, red lead: On top of the St. Olov’s crown and socks/shoes, lips
  • Light brown: Fur imitation on the hem of the garment
  • Medium Brown: Hair and beard
  • Dark brown: On top of the crown of the human-headed dragon under St. Olov’s feet
  • White: in the eyes
  • Black: in the eyes

Silvering: Silvering together with probably with a red lake glaze on the body of the human-headed dragon under St. Olov’s feet.

Hook on the back: There is an original hook on the back of the sculpture.

Other comments: There is a heavily darkened old retouching on the left side of the garment.
